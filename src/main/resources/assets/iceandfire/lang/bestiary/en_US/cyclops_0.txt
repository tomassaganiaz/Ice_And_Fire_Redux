Woe to those who explore the
expanses of the world's beaches,
for a beast taller than a dragon
lurks in the sea-side caves.

The Cyclops is a monstrous beast
with a single eye. Although it is
fearsome to nearly all living
things, Cyclopes make skilled
shepherds. Cyclopes can often be
found guarding decent sized flocks
of sheep.

Most Cyclopes make their homes
from dug-out sea caves on the
shore. Inside these caves lay
both remnants of the cyclopes'
last meals and significant
treasures, aside from sheep.











Cyclopes are very vigilant
creatures, able to see
potential prey and threats
from afar. However, due to
them having only one eye,
this ability can be severely
dampened.
